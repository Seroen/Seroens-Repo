Welcome to Solar's Warehouse, formally known as the Modded Regions Starter Pack.

This is a site to help all level editors who desire to mod Rain World.

This site has mostly custom assets that have been collected over the years, and almost every single region to exist in the community, excluding some that got lost to time.

It also has the Vanilla assets if you don't want any of the custom ones.

And don't forget to have fun, or try to!

Click on the images to download things!
